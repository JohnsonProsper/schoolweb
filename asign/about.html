About Us

About Us:
Zibeh Institute of Technology established in 2007, consists of students and staff from different parts of Nigeria. The Institute runs various programmes which have attracted lots of students from within and the neighbouring countries. ZIT is mandated by Nigeria’s Federal Ministry of Education via the National Board for Technical Education(NBTE) to promote information technology(IT) in four specialized fields: Computer Software Engineering, Computer Hardware Engineering, Network and System Security and Multimedia Technology. ZIT’s mandate also includes the development of young entrepreneurs whose acquired knowledge would provoke desire for creativity, innovation, inventiveness and affinity for higher education. As students, we engage them in rudimentary creativity and inventions as well as imbue them with knowledge and skills on entrepreneurship. This, it is hoped, would help them become self-employed when they establish small scale enterprises. The diploma awarded by the Institute is accredited and therefore a Direct-Entry(DE) requirement by JAMB for candidates seeking admission to university.

values

Our core value at zibeh institute of technology:

- Excellence: We will strive for the highest standards of teaching, research, and service.
- Integrity: We will always act with honesty and respect, upholding the highest ethical standards.
- Innovation: We will encourage creativity and risk-taking, in pursuit of new ideas and solutions.
- Service: We will serve our students, our community, and our world, using our knowledge and resources to make a positive impact.
- Diversity: We will celebrate and value the unique perspectives and experiences of our students,
- Collaboration: We will work together, across disciplines and backgrounds, to achieve our goals.
- Accountability: We will take responsibility for our actions and hold ourselves to the highest standards of performance.
- Stewardship: We will be responsible and ethical in the use of our resources, including financial, physical, and human resources.
- Transparency: We will be open and honest in our communication and decision-making.
